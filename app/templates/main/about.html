Who We Are

Swahili Afro Jamboree

Swahili Afro Jamboree (SAJ) is a social platform brought into existence for a purpose of bringing together Swahili speakers and non-Swahili speakers around the globe for sharing, learning, promoting, celebrating, and educating each other about Swahili culture.

Founded in 2024 in the United States, our organization is dedicated to creating meaningful cultural exchange through events, educational initiatives, and community building.

Bringing people together through culture

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To bring and spread Swahili culture to the world

"Connecting cultures, celebrating heritage, and building global understanding"

Our Mission

The mission of Swahili Afro Jamboree is to enable intercultural relationship between Swahili and other cultures at a global stage in various cultural aspects including Swahili knowledge, food, music and dancing, fashion, and showcasing of Swahili trading through gathering, promotion and celebration.

"Creating spaces for learning, sharing, and celebrating the richness of Swahili culture"

Our Initiatives

What We Do

We focus our efforts on spreading Swahili culture to the world community through various initiatives and activities.

Our activities are designed to foster deep understanding and appreciation of Swahili culture while creating meaningful connections across cultural boundaries.

Our Objectives & Goals

Primary Objective

Bringing together Swahili speakers and non-Swahili speakers from around the globe for a purpose of sharing, interacting, learning, promoting, celebrating, and educating each other about Swahili culture.

Long-term Goal

Supporting global efforts aimed at spreading and increasing deep understanding of Swahili culture and growing Swahili speakers from current 230 million to 300 million by 2030.
